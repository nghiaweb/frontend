Natural gemstones

We at Cloister’s refer to natural gemstones as gems which are free of any intervention by humans apart of cutting and polishing. Therefore certificates issued by well-known gemological laboratories will accompany all gems.

Quality

Cloisters House Limited refers to gems as a form of investment, we only recommend this investment when the stones possess very the very fine qualities found in gemstones.

Size

We only suggest gems of a certain size. For example a sapphire, regarded as investment, should be at least 2 or 3 carats, even if the gem is natural and of fine quality. Rubies and diamonds should be 1 carat and up. The combination of the three characteristics mentioned above ensures that the gem is rare. Rarity is a very important factor when considering a gemstone
